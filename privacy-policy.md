# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

Duddy Portugues is a Brazilian Portuguese learning app that uses local learning content, speech practice, optional AI coaching, and pronunciation assessment.

## Information We Collect

- Account information: email address and authentication identifiers when you create an account.
- Learning activity: lessons started, phrases practiced, review grades, favorites, streaks, XP, daily goal progress, and usage counters.
- Audio data: short speech recordings may be sent to the pronunciation service for scoring when you choose to record.
- AI coach inputs: target phrase, transcript, pronunciation score, and weak phoneme data may be sent to the AI coach when you request feedback.
- Diagnostics: crash logs, device information, and basic analytics may be collected to improve reliability.

## How We Use Information

We use your information to provide language learning features, save progress, sync account data, assess pronunciation, generate coaching feedback, enforce quotas, detect abuse, and improve app quality.

## Service Providers

The app may use:

- Supabase for authentication and cloud profile data.
- Azure Speech Services for pronunciation assessment.
- OpenAI or another configured AI provider for coaching feedback.
- Firebase services for crash reporting and basic analytics if enabled.

Audio recordings are processed for pronunciation assessment and are not intended to be stored permanently by Duddy Portugues.

## Data Retention

Local progress remains on your device unless you uninstall the app or clear app data. Account and cloud usage data are retained while your account is active or as needed for security, billing, legal compliance, and abuse prevention.

## Your Choices

You may request account deletion, data export, or correction by contacting support. You can also avoid audio or AI processing by not using recording, pronunciation assessment, or AI coach features.

## Children

Duddy Portugues is not intended for children under 13.

## Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards, but no system is perfectly secure. Do not submit sensitive medical, legal, financial, or confidential information to the AI coach.

## Contact

For privacy requests, contact: support@duddy.app
